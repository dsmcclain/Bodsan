ABOUT ME

My name is Dylan, and I started BODSAN because I was tired of all of the nonsense our culture sustains when it comes to bodies. My own body is white, cis, hetero, able, and thin, and as a result I live with many, many layers of privilege. I have benefitted, in ways seen and unseen, from the injustices of our society and from our culture's obsession with valuing and devaluing bodies. My goal with BODSAN is to be a voice of dissent from within this culture, and to boost the voices of those who are actively dismantling it, often from positions of less privilege than my own. There is no wrong way to have a body. Anything else is just body nonsense.
